About Manipur

Welcome to Manipur, a jeweled land nestled in a lush green corner of Northeast India. Often called the "Switzerland of the East," this vibrant state is a melting pot of culture and tradition, where ancient history and breathtaking natural beauty coexist.

It is famously known as the birthplace of modern Polo, a testament to its proud and spirited history. From its classical dance forms to its unique cuisine and rare flora and fauna, Manipur offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.
